Why Driveway Cleaning Matters

A clean driveway is one of the fastest ways to boost curb appeal. Dirt, oil, and algae build up over time — especially in North Georgia's humid climate.

How Often Should You Clean It?

For most homeowners, once a year is enough. If you live near trees or have heavy shade, you might need it twice a year.

Signs It's Time

  • Green or black algae growth
  • Oil stains from leaking vehicles
  • Weeds growing through cracks
  • Noticeable discoloration
